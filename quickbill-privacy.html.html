Privacy Policy

QuickBill: Invoice Maker by NeriLabs

Effective date: March 1, 2026

QuickBill is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe your business data belongs to you and only you.

Data Storage

All data you create in QuickBill is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Client information (names, email addresses, physical addresses)
  • Invoice data (amounts, line items, dates, notes)
  • Your business information and settings

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third party. We do not have servers that store your data.

No Data Collection

QuickBill does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal or business data. We do not use analytics services, tracking pixels, advertising frameworks, or any form of user monitoring.

PDF Generation and Sharing

Invoice PDFs are generated entirely on your device. PDF files are only shared when you explicitly choose to share them using the iOS share sheet. We have no access to the PDFs you create or share.

Subscriptions and Payments

QuickBill offers optional paid subscriptions processed through the App Store. Two third-party services are involved in this process:

  • Apple processes all payments and manages subscriptions through the App Store. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat helps us manage subscription status. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

No Account Required

QuickBill does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

Children's Privacy

QuickBill is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of QuickBill after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
