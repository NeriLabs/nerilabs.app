Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

NeriLabs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the TipJar mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

TipJar stores your tip data (amounts, dates, job types, and notes) locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Subscription Data

If you subscribe to TipJar Pro, your purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier to track your subscription — no personal information such as your name, email, or payment details is shared with us or RevenueCat.

Analytics & Tracking

TipJar does not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or advertising frameworks. We do not collect usage data, device information, or behavioral analytics.

Data Storage

All tip data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed. We do not have access to your data at any time.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is RevenueCat for subscription management. You can review their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

TipJar is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@nerilabs.app.
