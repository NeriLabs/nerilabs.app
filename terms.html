Description of Service

TipJar is a personal income tracking application designed for tip-based workers including servers, bartenders, baristas, delivery drivers, and similar professionals. The App provides:

  • Quick tip logging and shift tracking
  • Tip calculator with bill splitting
  • Earnings dashboard with charts and statistics
  • Income goal setting and progress tracking
  • Data export to CSV and PDF formats (Pro feature)
  • Historical earnings data with date navigation (Pro feature)

Free and Pro Features

TipJar offers both free and paid features:

Free features include: tip calculator, up to 5 tip/shift entries per calendar month, basic dashboard with current week/month view, one daily income goal, and appearance customization.

TipJar Pro unlocks unlimited tip and shift logging, full earnings history, date navigation on charts, weekly and monthly goals, data export, and additional insights. Pro is available through auto-renewable subscriptions.
